Biography

I am currently a Research Associate in the Department of Biomedical Engineering, Case Western Reserve University, working with Shuo Li. My research lies in biomedical informatics, computer vision, and deep learning, focusing on efficient medical image representation for diagnosis, surgery, and imaging. I have published 20+ peer-reviewed papers (RBME, CVPR, ECCV, TNNLS, MedIA, NC, TIP, J-BHI, IJCAI, MICCAI, IPMI, etc.). Organizer of MICCAI-KiPA22 challenge, reviewer for J-BHI, TMI, CVPR, ICCV, MICCAI, AAAI, NeurIPS.
